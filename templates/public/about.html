About RoutePlanner

Exploring the world through algorithms and graphs

Our Mission

RoutePlanner is an academic initiative designed to demonstrate the power of Graph Algorithms in real-world mapping scenarios. By utilizing real geographic nodes and edges, we calculate optimal routes tailored to various transportation modes. Our goal is not just to show a path, but to visualize the underlying logic of modern navigation systems.
